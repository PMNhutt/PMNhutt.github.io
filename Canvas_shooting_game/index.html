The earth is in danger!

A huge amount of meteoroids is heading straight to the Earth! We have to destroy it before it go through the atmosphere and ruin everything! but the meteoroids seem not normal...

Elementals & Enemies

After analyzing we discovered that there are 7 elements (left) and the meteoroids (right) also have 7 types corresponding to the natural elements.

Elemental Reactions

Elemental Reactions are triggered by applying different elemental on a target. On the left is the strong elemental reactions which will cause more damage to the enemy, also the weak reaction on the right will cause less damage

Controls

Use keyboard to switch projectile type. click to shoot!

Destroy it all!

Using the right elemental reaction will help you deal with it easy (left) or else it will take more time to make it explode (right)

Score count

normal is +10 for every meteoroid destroyed but when you destroy a meteoroid with strong elemental reaction you'll get bonus +20 score. head up!!! if your projectile type is match (same element) with the meteoroid immune effect occured which -5 your score on hit
